Credits

Shrek is a movie directed by two persons - Andrew Adamson and Vicky Jenson. It was animated by DreamWorks Animation and PDI/DreamWorks. Our beloved ogre was voiced by Mike Myers. Donkey was voiced by Eddie Murphy, Fiona was voiced by Cameron Diaz and Lord Farquaad was voiced by John Lithgow.
